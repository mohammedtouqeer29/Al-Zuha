NUZHA HAJJ PACKAGE

Package Overview

This Nuzha Hajj Group package includes a well-organized stay in Makkah before and after Hajj, along with complete arrangements during the days of Mina, Arafat, and Muzdalifah.

  • Makkah Stay: 10 days (10 days before Hajj & 10 days after Hajj), approximately 3.5 km from Haram Shareef (Nuzha)
  • Hajj Days: 5 days stay in Mina, Arafat, and Muzdalifah with necessary arrangements
  • Room Arrangement: Sharing basis (4–5 persons per room)
  • Transportation: Air-conditioned buses with seat-by-seat allocation
  • Madinah Stay:9 days in Madina Munawara
  • Meals: Hyderabadi-style food provided
  • Extras: Local Ziyarat, complimentary hand bag & shoulder bag
